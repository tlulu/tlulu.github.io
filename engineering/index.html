HOME VPN

During my travels abroad, I wanted to access websites which were blocked in certain countries. A VPN provider like NordVPN would do the job, but I wanted something with more privacy. Historically, there have been data breaches within VPN providers. It’s hard to trust whether VPN providers actually keep logs, sell our data or monitor our traffic. It’s totally possible that if they can profit or have to protect themselves, they with share them with other companies.

CONNEXTOR

I co-founded ConNextor with Elva Young and David Song during ours studies at the University of Waterloo. The mission was to connect students from different disciplines to collaborate on projects together. Hackathons are a great platform to meet people and hack on things in a short time frame, but we found that most projects stopped progress after the hackathon. We wanted to foster collaboration for longer term projects. ConNextor was a social platform for students to gain and track experiences by collaborating on projects together.
