👋 I’m Di (Tony) Lu

I am currently helping to redefine people’s relationship with money via Cash App. I focused on building investing and Bitcoin financial products for consumers.

I have 7+ years of software engineering experience and have early stage startup experience. I graduated from the University of Waterloo with a bachelors in Software Engineering

My entrepreneurial fire started back in college where I cofounded a social platform company with friends. Later, I worked closely with the founders of Avidbots, a 10 person robotics startup. Both experiences were meaningful and transformative. I love building impactful products with small close knit teams.

My goal in life is simple: live everyday to its fullest and go beyond my comfort zone each day. I want to keep growing and pushing my boundaries to create meaningful things that will help people.

Even though I’ve spent a lot of time learning how computers work, I am MORE obsessed with what makes people tick. I’m passionate about interpersonal dynamics and the blueprints of exceptional team cultures. At the end of the day, I want to find out everyone’s superpower. I provide mentorship on ADPList.

I’m excited about:

  • Building an open space community dedicated to open-ended curiosity, introspection and deep personal connection
  • Technology that improves the health and wellbeing of humans
  • The future of money, self custody and economic impact of BTC and stable coins
  • Doing a pistol squat and muscle up 💪

If any of those resonate, please reach out!
